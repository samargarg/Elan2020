The Domains

Sponsorship

This domain mainly focuses on :

  • Developing fund-raising plans.
  • Identify potential sources of funds.
  • Maintain relationships with Sponsors.

Public Relations

This domain mainly focuses on :

  • Analysing public opinion and drafting efficient strategies.
  • Social Media Management
  • Content Development

Techy

The roles of this domain are :

  • Organising various technical competitions.
  • Ensuring that all necessary arrangements are made for technical events.

Infrastructure

This is responsible for

  • Providing the required stuff for other domains
  • Hassle free conduction of workshop
  • Room Bookings, Assisting Instructors and Artists, etc.

Workshops

This domain mainly deals with :

  • Conducting workshops in association with various companies
  • Coordinating with faculty and students who attend workshops.

Web

The members of this domain :

  • Develop the official website for Elan and ηVision.
  • Regularly add all updates to the website.

Finances

The responsibilities of of this domain include :

  • Distributing the all funds as per requirement.
  • Coordinating with other domains and ensuring that all the funds are used properly.

Pronites

This domain is responsible for escorting chief guests and celebrities.

Social Cause

  • Every year Elan and ηVision is associated with a particular social cause.
  • This domain ensures that events which promote the social cause are organized.

Creatives

Creatives domain is responsible for :

  • Sprucing the aesthetics of the fest by their alluring props and creativity.
  • This just makes the fest extra fun and enjoyable

Informals

  • Informals is indeed one of the main things that brings about better interaction of the outside participants and organizers
  • Makes them feel at ease with fun events and contests.

Publicity

How can a fest be a success without good reach? That's where the publicity team steps in and makes sure many people know about the fest to increase the number of participants.

Multimedia

  • Our fest surely has a myriad of emotional, vulnerable and amazing moments.
  • We have our multimedia team to capture those moments and to make amazing teasers and videos.

Transport

During offline events it is one which arranges transport to our campus from hyd for workshops and fest.

Litr

Litr is in charge of all the literary events of the fest. This includes conducting events like quizzes and debates on the day of the fest, along with the online LitFest which usually happens a few months before the fest. But the most anticipated event by Litr is of course, our very own Online Treasure Hunt, Cryptex.

EML

Nothing can be as enriching as bringing in some notable personalities to have a nice talk and an interactive session. So Extra mural lectures domain takes care of entertainment plus education.

Culti

With the sole motive of entertaining the audience, CULTI promises to enthral the audience with an ensemble of out-of-this world performances.

Biggies

Experience the heat of the dazzling array of shows that set the stage on fire.

Hospitality

  • Ensuring accommodation is provided to guests and students.
  • Confirming whether all the basic amenities are supplied.
  • Managing venues for various events/competitions.

Security

  • When celebrities come this domain takes care of their safety.
  • They also make sure that workshops go smoothly without any hassles.
