Elan and ηVision is the annual techno-cultural fest of IIT Hyderabad and is one of the largest fests  in South India. Elan refers to the cultural part and Nvision cites the technological part of fest. It is a 3-day long fest which is held every year in the month of February. This fest offers ample opportunities to students who wish to showcase their expertise in technological domains and artistry in cultural counterparts.  Students hail from various institutes throughout the country and take away bagful memories and experiences.

About Us


This fest is held in the campus of IIT Hyderabad and is  completely organized by the students. Right after the fest gets completed, preparations for the next year’s fest begin. Students of IITH amalgamate into a team and put all their efforts into organizing this fest. The whole organizing team is divided into 19 domains. Each domain has its own motive and functionality. All the domains coordinate with each other and host this fabulous fest.
